Fried Oreos

"You haven't lived until you've had a fried oreo"

Ingredients you will need:

AmountIngredient
1Package of Oreo Cookies
2Eggs
2Cups Bisquick
1 1/2Cups Milk
3Teaspoons Vegetable Oil
  1. Preheat the deepfryer to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix the Bisquick, eggs, milk, and oil
  3. Dip and cover Oreos completely in this mixture
  4. Place in hot oil of deepfryer
  5. Flip Oreo when bottom side is brown (and repeat)

Bon appetit!

All About the Fried Oreo

The Fried Oreo is an American delicacy. But how did it come into being? Legend has it that Charlie Boghosian first popularized the creation at a fair in just 2002. Since then, the Fried Oreo has become quite the delicacy, cult favorite, and festival staple. Coated in sweet, fluffy, fried batter with a decadent, soft Oreo filling, the Fried Oreo has become famous and a fan favorite.

Despite its good looks and heavenly taste, however, the Fried Oreo has a dark side: its nutritional value, or lack thereof. According to CalorieKing.com, an average Fried Oreo contains about 51 grams of sugar, 108 carbs, and 48 grams of total fat. (More information below)

The beauty of the Fried Oreo should be enjoyed, simply in moderation. Save this sweet treat for special occasions and celebrations, and do not have too much of a good great thing. When you look for a sweet crowd pleaser at your next event, think Fried Oreo. It will not disappoint.
